Early Settlements of Rome

The beginning of Rome is rooted in both myth and history.

Monarchy To Republic

The Roman Republic was founded in 509 BCE after the overthrow of the Roman monarchy, marked by the expulsion of the last king, Tarquin the Proud.

Roman Empire at its peak

The Roman Empire began in 27 BCE when Augustus became the first Roman Emperor (Caesar).

Christianity in Roman Empire

The rise of Christianity in the Roman Empire began in the 1st century CE with the teachings of Jesus of Nazareth and his followers, who spread the message of salvation, love, and faith in one God.

The fall of Roman Empire

The fall of the Western Roman Empire traditionally occurred in 476 CE when the last Roman emperor, Romulus Augustulus, was deposed by the Germanic chieftain Odoacer.
